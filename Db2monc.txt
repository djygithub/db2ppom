      ******************************************************************
      *
      *    NOTE - THIS ROUTINE RETURNS GREENWICH MEAN TIME, WHICH IS
      *           NOT NECESSARILY LOCAL TIME.  THIS IS A STCK
      *           LIMITATION (FEATURE?)
      *
      *
      *    (1)  INITIALIZE BASE DATE TO 00.00 HOURS, JANUARY 1, 1988
      *
      *    (2)  COMPUTE TIME BETWEEN NOW AND BASE DATE
      *
      *    (3)  COMPUTE # YEARS BETWEEN BASE DATE AND NOW
      *
      *    (4)  ADD # YEARS TO BASE
      *
      *    (5)  COMPUTE JULIAN DAY OF YEAR
      *
      *    (6)  COMPUTE HOURS AND MINUTES
      *
      *    (7)  COMPUTE SECONDS
      *
      ******************************************************************
       CONVERT-TIME-STAMP.
      *(1)
           MOVE 1988 TO YEAR.
      *    MOVE 1 TO DAYS.
      *(2)
           SUBTRACT 1988-YEAR FROM TIME-WORK GIVING TIME-WORK-1.
      *(3)
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-1 BY 1461-DAYS GIVING TIME-WORK-2
               REMAINDER TIME-WORK-3.
           IF  TIME-WORK-2 > 0
               COMPUTE YEAR = (YEAR + (4 * TIME-WORK-2)).
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-3 BY 365-DAYS GIVING TIME-WORK-4
               REMAINDER TIME-WORK-5.
      *(4)
           IF TIME-WORK-4 > 0
               COMPUTE YEAR = YEAR + TIME-WORK-4.
      *(4A)
           DIVIDE YEAR BY 4 GIVING TIME-WORK-10
               REMAINDER TIME-WORK-11.
           IF  TIME-WORK-11 = 0
               MOVE 1 TO DAYS
           ELSE
               MOVE 0 TO DAYS.
      *(5)
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-5 BY 1-DAY GIVING TIME-WORK-6
               REMAINDER TIME-WORK-7.
           COMPUTE DAYS = DAYS + TIME-WORK-6.
      *(6)
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-7 BY 1-MINUTE GIVING TIME-WORK-8
               REMAINDER TIME-WORK-9.
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-8 BY 60 GIVING HOURS
               REMAINDER MINUTES.
      *(7)
           DIVIDE TIME-WORK-9 BY 1-SECOND GIVING SECONDS.
